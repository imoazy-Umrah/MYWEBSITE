Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your Watamu holiday.

What does 279 EUR per day include? ▾
The 279 EUR/day package includes beachfront accommodation at a 5-star hotel (Garoda or Kobe Suit Resort), airport transfers from Mombasa or Malindi, daily breakfast, access to marine park tours, a dedicated driver, and 24/7 concierge. Safari and lunch/dinner can be added.
What is the minimum stay? ▾
Minimum 5 nights, starting from 1,395 EUR per person. We recommend 7–10 nights to combine beach relaxation with a Tsavo East safari extension (2–3 nights).
Which airport should I fly into? ▾
Malindi Airport (MYD) is just 20 minutes from Watamu. Mombasa Moi International Airport (MBA) is 1.5 hours away. Both have private transfers included.
Can I add a safari to my Watamu holiday? ▾
Yes. Tsavo East National Park is 2–3 hours from Watamu. We arrange 2-3 night extensions with lodge accommodation, game drives, and park fees. See safari costs →
Is Watamu safe for tourists? ▾
Watamu is very safe. The local community is welcoming, resorts have security, and our drivers/guides ensure your safety. Take normal precautions (avoid empty beaches at night, lock valuables).
