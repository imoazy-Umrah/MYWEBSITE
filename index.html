✈️ Kenya eTA (Electronic Travel Authorisation) – What You Need
Most nationalities require an eTA before travelling to Kenya. It costs $32.50 USD and is approved within 2‑5 days. Important: The eTA itself is NOT a visa. Your actual 90‑day stay permission is granted free of charge by immigration officers at the airport upon arrival. The eTA only allows you to board the flight.
