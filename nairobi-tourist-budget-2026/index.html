Must‑See Attractions – Premium Experiences

🦒

Giraffe Centre (Private Visit)

Cost: KES 1,500 (~€12) Best time: 9–10am (less crowded). Feed endangered Rothschild giraffes from a raised platform. Private guided visits available.

🐘

Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage

Cost: $20 donation. Open: 11am–12pm daily. Booking essential. Watch baby elephants being fed – an exclusive and heartwarming experience.

🦁

Nairobi National Park – Private Safari

Cost: $80 park fee. Best time: 6–9am or late afternoon. Exclusive 4x4 safari with private guide – see lions, rhinos, and zebras with the city skyline.

🏛️

Karen Blixen Museum

Cost: KES 550 (~€4). Colonial‑era home of "Out of Africa" author. Private historian guide available.

🌿

Karura Forest – Private Nature Walk

Cost: KES 600 (~€5). 30km of walking/biking trails, waterfalls, caves. Private nature guide available on request.

🛍️

Maasai Market – Private Shopping Tour

Free entry. Bargaining essential. Best days: Tuesday (CBD) and Saturday (KICC roof). Private shopping guide available to help you navigate and negotiate.

Frequently Asked Questions – Nairobi Luxury Travel

Is Nairobi safe for tourists? ▾
Yes – Nairobi is safe in tourist areas (Karen, Gigiri, Westlands). We arrange private transfers for all our guests and provide 24/7 concierge support.
How many days do I need in Nairobi? ▾
2–3 days is ideal for a luxury stopover: one day for the elephant orphanage + giraffe centre, another day for Nairobi National Park and Karen Blixen Museum.
What is the best neighbourhood to stay in Nairobi? ▾
Karen and Gigiri are the safest and most peaceful – home to top luxury hotels like Hemingways Nairobi and Giraffe Manor.
Can I see the Big Five in Nairobi National Park? ▾
Yes – you can see lions, leopards, rhinos, elephants, and buffalo. It's one of only two national parks worldwide where you can see the Big Five inside a capital city.
What is the best time to visit Nairobi? ▾
June–October (dry, cooler) is ideal for safaris. December–March (warm, dry) is great for a beach + city combo.
Do I need a visa for Kenya? ▾
Most nationalities require an e‑visa (electronic Travel Authorisation). Apply online at etakenya.go.ke. Fee $32.50. Processing takes 2–5 days.
