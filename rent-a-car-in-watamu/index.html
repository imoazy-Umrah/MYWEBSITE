Frequently Asked Questions — Car Rental Watamu

❓ Do I need a 4x4 in Watamu?
For driving around Watamu town, a sedan or compact is fine. For safari trips to Tsavo East, Gede Ruins, or Marafa, we recommend a 4x4 pickup for comfort on rough roads.
❓ Can I rent a car without a driver?
Yes – self‑drive is available for all vehicles. We also offer a professional driver option if you prefer to sit back and enjoy the scenery.
❓ What is the minimum rental period?
We offer daily, weekly, and monthly rentals. Minimum rental is 1 day. Longer rentals get discounted rates.
❓ Do you deliver the car to my hotel?
Yes – we deliver free of charge to any hotel in Watamu and to Malindi Airport. For Mombasa Airport, an additional fee applies.
❓ What fuel policy do you have?
We use a full‑to‑full policy: you receive the car with a full tank and return it with a full tank. This is the fairest and most transparent system.
❓ Can I take the car to Tsavo East?
Yes – our vehicles are approved for safari trips. We recommend a 4x4 pickup for Tsavo East, but sedans can also handle the main roads. Park entry fees are not included.
