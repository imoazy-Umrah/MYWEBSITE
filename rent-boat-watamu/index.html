Frequently Asked Questions – Boat Rental Watamu

❓ Do I need a license to rent a boat?
No – all our rentals come with a professional captain. If you hold a valid skipper's license and would prefer to self‑drive, we can arrange that on request.
❓ What is included in the rental?
Each boat includes the vessel, captain, safety equipment, and life jackets. Snacks and soft drinks can be added. Please request a quote for full details.
❓ Can I rent fishing or diving equipment without a boat?
Yes – equipment can be rented separately. However, we recommend pairing it with a boat for the best experience.
❓ What if the weather is bad on my booked day?
We monitor conditions closely. If the weather is unsafe, we will offer a full refund or reschedule your booking at no extra charge.
❓ Where can we go with the boat?
Popular routes include Mida Creek (mangroves, bird watching), Watamu Marine Park (snorkeling, coral), and deep‑sea fishing grounds. We can customise your itinerary.
❓ How do I get a price?
Simply fill in the form below or chat with us on WhatsApp – we'll send you a custom quote based on your chosen boat, duration, and any extras.
